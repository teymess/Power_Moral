Before you start the game, please take note of the following information:

As previously mentioned, your payment consists of a fixed reward of $0.80 for successful completion of the HIT and a bonus that depends on the decision of your team and on the decisions of 9 other teams, each of which also have a manager and a worker. This means that your bonus depends on the decisions of a total of 20 different MTurkers.

Collecting this many HITs will take some time. Therefore, we will match your team with 9 other teams only after all assignments of this HIT are completed. This may take a couple of days. Once we have matched the teams, we will reward you with your bonus.

To proceed to the game, please click "Next".
