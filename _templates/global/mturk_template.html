This HIT is an academic research study on decision making.

Research goal:
In this study, we are interested in decision making in a dilemma situation.
You will be matched with co-players and you will be asked to make a decision that has financial consequences for you, your co-player, and other MTurkers.

Duration and reward:
The entire HIT will take about 10 minutes. Your payment consists of a fixed reward of $0.80 for successful completion and a bonus that depends on your own and your co-players’ decisions as well as on the decisions of other MTurkers.
You are also asked to complete a short survey.

Please note that the task should be completed without delay. Confidentiality:
All data that is collect is treated confidentially and will only be used for academic research purpose.
The entire HIT is anonymous.

Requirements:
To participate, you need to be located in the United States of America.
You may not have participated in this HIT before.
After reading the instructions, there will be a short comprehension quiz. If you do not pass this quiz, you cannot continue with the HIT and will receive no reward.
There are no other formal requirements for participation.

Voluntary participation:
Participation in this study is voluntary. If you do not want to take part in the study, please do not accept the HIT.
If you want to participate, please be sure you can commit to completing the HIT before accepting it - if you discontinue the task, you will not receive any bonus.

Contact:
If you have any questions regarding this study, please contact Prof. Dr. Florian Diekert: diekert@uni-heidelberg.de.
